Requests for Clients

DELETE	http://localhost:8080/ClientStore/rs/clients/1			delete a client id=1
POST	http://localhost:8080/ClientStore/rs/clients/			create a client
GET	http://localhost:8080/ClientStore/rs/clients/id/email/ivan.ivanov@@hotmail.com
GET	http://localhost:8080/ClientStore/rs/clients/id			findAll()
GET	http://localhost:8080/ClientStore/rs/clients/id/7		findById(7)
PUT	http://localhost:8080/ClientStore/rs/clients/id/10		update a client id=10
